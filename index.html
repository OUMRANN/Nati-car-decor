About Nati Car Decor

Where Premium Craftsmanship Meets Automotive Passion

Founded in the heart of Addis Ababa, Nati Car Decor has grown into a trusted name for drivers who expect more from their vehicle's interior. We believe every car deserves an interior as refined as its exterior — combining premium materials, precision installation, and an obsessive attention to detail.

From custom upholstery to the latest infotainment technology, our expert technicians work with a single goal: delivering vehicle aesthetics and comfort that exceed expectations, every single time.
